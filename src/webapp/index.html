About the solution

Machine Learning & Decision Optimization for the Swedish healthcare system

Perhaps the most important challenge we face right now is how to ensure that all our hospitals have access to an adequate amount of equipment and resources. Researchers suggest that a lack of ICU-beds, respirators and protective gears could mean a tenfolf increase in COVID-19 mortality rate.

Vårdoptimeraren is a two-pronged approach for ensuring that all Swedish regions have access to the equipment they need. By first applying machine learning to forecast the amount of future ICU cases in each region, we can see where to expect shortages. We then apply mathematical decision optimization techniques to get suggestions for the mathematically optimal way to move either patients or resources in order to avoid shortages and save lives.
